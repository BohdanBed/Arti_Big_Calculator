This letter (the “Facility Arrangement”) is delivered to you in connection with the Term Sheet herewith (together with Summary of Terms attached thereto, the “Term Sheet”) among you, Seneca West, LLC. (“Seneca West”) and Employer Retention Credit Facility (“RCF”) regarding the successful arrangement, underwriting and syndication of employer credit cash advance in an aggregate maximum principal amount not to exceed the total amount established for the employer retention tax credit (the “Acceleration Facility”). Unless otherwise defined herein, capitalized terms shall have the same meanings as specified therefor in the Terms Sheet. In connection with, and in consideration of the agreements contained in the Terms Sheet, you agree with Seneca

West as follows:

1. ARRANGEMENT FEE. You will pay to Seneca West, for its own account, a fee (the “Arrangement Fee”) of 0.50% of the gross amount employer retention credit acceleration. Such Arrangement Fee shall be for the structuring and syndication of the Acceleration Facility and shall be payable in full on the Closing Date.

2. ADMINISTRATIVE AGENCY FEE. You will pay a one time administrative agent fee of 0.75%, to Seneca West, for its own account as Administrative Agent for the Lenders under the Acceleration Facility, in full on the Closing Date(s).

3. Document Management FEES. You will pay to Seneca West, to its own account, fees (“Doc Fees”) of (a) 0.75% of the aggregate maximum principal amount allowed by the Acceleration Facility. Such Doc Fees shall be for the Seneca West participation in the Acceleration Facility document management and shall be payable in full upon the Closing Date (other than as to any increase, which Upfront Fees will be paid on the date of any such increase).
All of the fees described above in this letter agreement shall be fully earned upon becoming due and payable in accordance with the terms hereof, shall be nonrefundable for any reason whatsoever and shall be in addition to any other fees, costs and expenses payable pursuant to the Terms Sheet or the definitive documentation for the Acceleration Facility. Seneca West reserves the right to allocate, in whole or in part, certain fees payable to Seneca West hereunder in such manner as Seneca West and Acceleration Facility shall agree in their sole discretion.
Your obligation to pay the foregoing fees will not be subject to counterclaim or setoff for, or be otherwise affected by, any claim or dispute you may have.
